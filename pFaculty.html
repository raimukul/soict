One of the biggest strengths of the Univerity School of Information and Communication Technology is its dedicated, competent and accomplished faculty. The faculty members of the university are prepared to take an extra step that separates excellence from merely adequate. As this is a residential campus, the faculty members are available for consultations and guidance outside the classroom as well in an informal and customized level.
